Gnawing


Gentle and unassuming,
You go through each layer.
On my flesh you keep gnawing.
Your convenience is one I'll cater.

With a smile plastered on,
Your scheme cleverly sleight.
Can't be witnessed by anyone,
Despite being in broad daylight.

From the blood you call feast,
I avert my eyes.
I shake my leg like a freak.
Damn these stupid flies!
