It’s kinda funny really. I don’t know how and when, but I started to dislike birthdays. What was once a cheerful celebration, is now a bitter reminder of the hours I have wasted. Time is a harsh wave that will not wait on a speck of sand.

This unfortunately brings me to now. Here I am staring at my discolored laptop screen, encoding what I mindlessly scribbled on paper. Occasionally a tiny doodle occupies a portion of a page. This doodle is not much different from what I would make half a decade ago. That’s more the reason I hate it.

To think I’ve been drawing for years yet never made art. Or at the very least, made good art. While staring at the vast ocean, a feeling washed over me. An overwhelming feeling of fear whenever I was forced to move along the current. The gust of sea aggressively yells at me “Are you a better person than you were a year ago?”

To be honest, I don’t think so. Maybe that’s what’s wrong with me. I mean what am I even supposed to do in such a situation? Stand my ground against nature? Up, down, right, and left, I see that I am not the only grain of sand being dragged about. It is foolish of me to think I should be a special exemption to what is a common situation.

I think I’d be a little more happier if I learned to go with the flow.
