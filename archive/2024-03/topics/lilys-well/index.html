About

Tip-tapping along the window. The horrid sound of the rain’s drumming fills the room. On your bed you lay, struggling to fall asleep. Among nature’s serenade, a voice can be heard. The murmuring pleads of a child. You stare out the window. Could it be?

You quickly rose from your bed. Its seems that your search for a sturdy rope has begun. Father is going to be so upset when he finds out.

Lily’s well has a simple premise. You play as a little girl named Lily. She wants to get to the bottom of the well to save the unknown voice. She must explore around and gather materials you can use to climb down.

Join Lily’s misadventures through the murky rainy environment, as she discovers a truth she may have wished never to find.
