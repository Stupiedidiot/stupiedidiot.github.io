Raffina

  • Character type: Insecure Evil Rival

She has a one sided rivalry that is, although presented to be a silly gimmick, I've always personally headcanon as a deeper insight to her psyche. She acts condescending towards Amitie despite being weaker than her. In terms of magic she needs to use her pouch paired with martial arts to even reach the same level as her peers. It's been shown during Lemres' run in the 20th Anniversary that Raffina is extremly insecure about her reliance to the pouch and panics when Lemres points it out. I like to believe her egotism is nothing more but a defense mechanism of the truth she is too scared to come to terms with. Especially since she comes from a prestigious family. Aside from that, I love me a evil pink haired villain!!!
