I have a lot of inspirations and I can honestly go on and on about it forever. There's just so many talented people alive <3
But I'll try my best to restrain myself so I won't end up overfilling this page

Literal hat

Hat is the first person I think of when it comes to inspirations and I often wonder why he's even following back my account lol. They are an extremly talented animator that plays around a lot with perspective and other artstyles. He's honestly such a chill artist who clearly has a lot to say through his art. He's been through a lot and I'm anticpating each new peice he brings.
Back October his instagram got wrongfully banned and lately he hasn't been doing well. I hope he's okay :'((

  • Instagram: LiteralHat
  • Favorite Piece: 11pm
  • Website: LiteralHat.com
  • Youtube: LiteralHat

Mattias Pilhede

Ex-Obssesive-Weeaboo turned into an amazing story teller. Words cannot properly express how much I admire his way of story telling. He takes silly and goofy concepts and make them a strange rollercoasters of "what the hell hit me". There is a very homey and comforting feeling to his videos.

  • Youtube: Mattias Pilhede
  • Favorite Video: Beginner's Guid To Tea

Carles Dalmau

I found their account way back when webtoon held a contest and he made an entry called "Lucid Lucy". I love their artstyle and I aspire to one day reach to the level of detail and wonderful colorpallets he puts into backgrounds.

  • Instagram: Carles_Dalmau
  • Webtoon: Lucid Lucy

Uricksaladbar

He's one of my favorite video essayist. I found him through his detroit video and just immediatley fell in love with his videos. He has a very distinguishable style and clearly puts a lot of thought and passion to the topic of his videos. I wanna make video essays one day and I hope to reach the level of quality his work has.

  • Youtube: Uricksaladbar
  • Favorite Video: Detroit: Become Human - It's Bad

Brian David Gilbert

Used to work with polygon but recently quit so he can make more independent stuff. Honestly a really funny song writter and I just love the chaotic energy he and his sister reaks of.

  • Youtube: Brian David Gliber
  • Favorite Video: Shingle jingle
