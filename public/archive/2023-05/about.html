About me

>Where can I learn about your ocs?

Ha! Very bold of you to assume you can. I have an unhealthy amount of ocs. I've developed a fear of sharing about them, for reason that are too lengthy to explain here. I will make comics about them soon, I promise. xD


>What are your inspirations?

When it comes to my art specifically: Literal hat, Kittydoggo, Bryan Lee O'Malley, and Carles Dalmau comes to mind. I am inspired by a lot of things and it's difficult to pin point them all! I could go on and on but I think it's best to save those ramblings for another time.


>Do you do commissions?

I've done quite a hefty amount of them, but the paranoia never seems to go away. I tend to stress about whether or not my art is worth the price I give it. Feel free to personally ask me, but know that there is no guarantee.
