What a Phoenix Hears


Here and there,
Among the mountains and skies,
Where only few men can bear
I’ve seen it all a thousand time

Desperate to live forever
They won’t go far
Fearful of unfinished endeavors
Soon to join the weary stars

What will come of the years?
When the flame pans out
With nothing but tears
Where can they turn about?

Spending each day to please,
Even a phoenix needs time to ease
