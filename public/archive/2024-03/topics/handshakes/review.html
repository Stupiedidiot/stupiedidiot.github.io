Webmaster's Thoughts

Handshakes can be finished in about an hour or two. There isn’t much content beyond 30 levels but honestly for a free game that is a lot. Is it worth getting into? Sure! I mean what else is there to risk aside from a couple fellows' arms.

It’s one of those games with simple mechanics but effectively difficult executions. The pacing is just right and gives you enough leverage to learn the quirks of the game. It’s a very promising premise and I would love to see more of it in the extended version.

In the meantime, I will be going through the rest of the developer's works.
